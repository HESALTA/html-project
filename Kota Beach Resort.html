KOTA BEACH RESORT

Kota Beach Resort is a lovely destination in Santa Fe, Bantayan Island. Known for its white sandy beach and clear waters, it’s perfect for swimming and sunbathing. The resort offers comfortable rooms and a relaxing atmosphere, making it ideal for families and couples alike. Enjoy fresh seafood at the restaurant, or take part in fun activities like snorkeling and island hopping. Kota Beach Resort is a wonderful place to unwind and enjoy island life!

Exciting Activities at Kota Beach Resort

  • Beach Volleyball: Join in for some fun on the sand with friends and family!
  • Snorkeling Adventures: Discover vibrant marine life in the crystal-clear waters.
  • Island Hopping Tours: Explore nearby islands and hidden gems of Bantayan.
  • Water Sports: Engage in thrilling activities like kayaking and paddleboarding.
  • Local Cuisine: Savor fresh seafood and local delicacies at our beachside restaurant.

About Kota Beach Resort

Kota Beach Resort is a tropical paradise located in the heart of Santa Fe, Bantayan Island. Famous for its white sand beach, crystal-clear waters, and laid-back atmosphere, this resort is an ideal destination for beach lovers and nature enthusiasts. Guests can enjoy a range of activities, including island hopping, kayaking, beach volleyball, and snorkeling. The resort’s cozy accommodations are designed for comfort and relaxation, offering easy access to the beach. The on-site restaurant serves a variety of local dishes and international favorites, making it perfect for those seeking a culinary experience with a view.

Location

Kota Beach Resort is located in Santa Fe, Bantayan Island, a peaceful area known for its picturesque beaches and tranquil environment. Accessible via boat from Hagnaya Port, it's a short and scenic ride to the island. The resort's prime location makes it the perfect base for exploring the best of Bantayan Island’s natural beauty and vibrant culture.
