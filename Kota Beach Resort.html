KOTA BEACH RESORT

Kota Beach Resort is a lovely destination in Santa Fe, Bantayan Island. Known for its white sandy beach and clear waters, it’s perfect for
swimming and sunbathing. The resort offers comfortable rooms and a relaxing atmosphere, making it ideal for families and couples alike.
Enjoy fresh seafood at the restaurant, or take part in fun activities like snorkeling and island hopping. Kota Beach Resort is a wonderful
place to unwind and enjoy island life!
