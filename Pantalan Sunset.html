BANTAYAN SEAWALL

In Bantayan Seawall, also known as the Pantalan, you can see beautiful sunsets. It's a great place to relax and enjoy the view as the sky changes colors.
You can sit on a bench or walk along the seawall, feeling the nice ocean breeze. It's perfect for taking a break and enjoying nature. In this spot, you
can bring a friend or loved one to share the moment and make wonderful memories as the sun sets in the distance.
