Welcome to Bantayan Island!

Bantayan Island is a beautiful and peaceful destination known for its white-sand beaches and clear blue waters. It’s an ideal place to relax and enjoy nature, whether you’re swimming, sunbathing, or simply admiring the view. One of the best experiences here is watching the stunning sunsets, when the sky turns vibrant shades of red, orange, and pink as the sun sets over the ocean. The island is also home to friendly locals who are always ready to share stories about their culture and traditions.


Apart from the amazing beaches, Bantayan Island offers many other activities. You can explore charming villages, enjoy fresh local food, or go on a boat ride to nearby islands. Whether you’re seeking adventure or just a peaceful escape, Bantayan has something for everyone. With its natural beauty, warm atmosphere, and plenty of things to do, Bantayan Island is a perfect place to unwind and create lasting memories.

Things to Visit in Bantayan

Bantayan Seawall

Experience the beautiful sunset views and the gentle waves at the Seawall.

Bantayan Island Plaza

Discover local shops and cultural events at the heart of the island.

Bantayan Church

Visit the historical Sts. Peter and Paul Church, a symbol of Bantayan's heritage.

Colina Isla De Bantayan

Enjoy panoramic views of the island from this beautiful hilltop location.
