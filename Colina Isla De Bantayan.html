COLINA ISLA DE BANTAYAN

Colina Isla De Bantayan is a stunning paradise on Bantayan Island, offering breathtaking views that stretch far and wide. For a modest entrance fee of Php 50.00, you can enjoy the beautiful scenery and relax in stylish cottages designed for comfort. Imagine lounging in a hammock, breathing in the fresh, salty air, and soaking up the sun. If you're a dog lover, you'll be delighted by the friendly dogs nearby, happily playing in their pen and eager for attention. This peaceful spot is perfect for unwinding, connecting with nature, and enjoying quality time with loved ones, making it a must-visit destination on the island.

Activities at Colina Isla De Bantayan

Colina Isla De Bantayan offers a range of activities for visitors to enjoy:

  • Hiking: Explore the natural surroundings and get breathtaking views of the island.
  • Swimming: Relax and cool off in the crystal-clear waters around the area.
  • Photography: Capture the stunning scenery and wildlife in the area.
  • Picnicking: Enjoy a picnic with friends or family in the scenic surroundings.

Upcoming Events

  • Island Cleanup: Monthly community event to clean and preserve the island (First Saturday of every month).
  • Yoga Retreat: A weekend yoga retreat in the beautiful nature of Colina Isla (Next Retreat: January 20-22).
