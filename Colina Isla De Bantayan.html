COLINA ISLA DE BANTAYAN

Colina Isla De Bantayan is a stunning paradise on Bantayan Island, offering breathtaking views that stretch far and wide. For a modest entrance fee
of Php 50.00, you can enjoy the beautiful scenery and relax in stylish cottages designed for comfort. Imagine lounging in a hammock, breathing in the
fresh, salty air, and soaking up the sun. If you're a dog lover, you'll be delighted by the friendly dogs nearby, happily playing in their pen and eager
for attention. This peaceful spot is perfect for unwinding, connecting with nature, and enjoying quality time with loved ones, making it a must-visit
destination on the island.
