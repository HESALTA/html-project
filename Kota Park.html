KOTA PARK

Kota Park in Madridejos, Bantayan Island, is a beautiful place by the sea. It has nice gardens, walking paths, and great views of the ocean.
The park is perfect for picnics and relaxing with family and friends. You can also see some historical buildings and enjoy the local vibe.
It's a lovely spot to unwind and have fun on your Bantayan trip!
