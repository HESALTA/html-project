KOTA PARK

Kota Park in Madridejos, Bantayan Island, is a serene place by the sea. It offers lush gardens, peaceful walking paths, and scenic views of the ocean.
The park is an ideal spot for relaxing, having a family picnic, and enjoying the local atmosphere. You’ll also find a few historical sites scattered throughout the area.
Whether you're a nature lover or someone who enjoys quiet moments, Kota Park is the perfect escape during your trip to Bantayan Island!

What to Do at Kota Park

Explore the lush gardens and enjoy the fresh sea breeze as you walk along the picturesque paths.

Perfect for families—have a delightful picnic with beautiful views of the ocean.

Photographers will love the park for its historical structures and natural beauty, perfect for capturing stunning photos.
