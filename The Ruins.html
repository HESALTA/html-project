The Ruins

The Ruins in Santa Fe is a must-see attraction on Bantayan Island. These iconic remnants of a historical estate have become a symbol of the island's past. Located amidst lush greenery and picturesque landscapes, The Ruins provide a fascinating glimpse into the local heritage and history. Whether you're an avid history enthusiast, an architect lover, or simply someone who appreciates beautiful, tranquil spots, The Ruins offers something for everyone.

Did You Know?

The Ruins were built in the early 1900s by a wealthy local family. The original structure was a grand estate, but it was destroyed during the Japanese occupation in World War II. Despite the destruction, The Ruins remain a significant historical site, providing insight into the island's rich cultural past. Today, visitors from all over the world visit the site to appreciate both the history and the stunning natural surroundings.

Things to Do Around The Ruins

  • Photography: The Ruins offer incredible opportunities for photography, with its unique architectural features, surrounded by lush greenery and a peaceful atmosphere. It’s an Instagram-worthy location for any traveler.
  • Guided Tours: Learn more about the history of The Ruins by joining a guided tour. Local guides provide valuable insights into the history and significance of the site.
  • Picnicking: There are designated areas where visitors can enjoy a peaceful picnic surrounded by nature. It’s a perfect way to unwind after exploring the site.
  • Exploration: Wander around the ruins and appreciate the detailed craftsmanship of the remaining walls, arches, and pillars. It's a peaceful retreat where you can connect with both nature and history.
