BANTAYAN PLAZA

In Bantayan Plaza, you can unwind, relax, and enjoy the local vibe. You can grab some tasty street food and stroll through the shops, taking in the sights
and sounds around you. It's a great place to meet friendly locals and experience the island's culture. In this lively spot, you can enjoy a snack while
soaking up the atmosphere, making it perfect for a laid-back day.
