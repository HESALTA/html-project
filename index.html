Welcome to Bantayan Island!
This beautiful place in the Philippines has white-sand beaches and clear blue waters. It's a great spot to relax,
enjoy tasty seafood, and watch stunning sunsets. You can explore friendly villages and have fun with different
activities. Whether you want peace or adventure, Bantayan Island is the perfect getaway!

Top Activities in Bantayan Island

  • Relax on the white sand beaches
  • Snorkel in crystal-clear waters
  • Visit historical landmarks like the Sts. Peter and Paul Church
  • Take a boat tour around the island
  • Enjoy fresh seafood at local restaurants

Best Time to Visit Bantayan Island

  • The dry season (December to May) is the best time to visit for sunny weather.
  • Expect fewer tourists during the rainy season (June to November), but check the weather first!
  • The island is especially beautiful during Christmas and New Year festivities.

Getting to Bantayan Island

  • Fly into Cebu City, then take a bus or taxi to Hagnaya Port (2-3 hours).
  • From Hagnaya Port, take a ferry (1 hour) to Bantayan Island.
  • Alternatively, you can take a direct flight to Bantayan Island via a private charter.
